Proximity

Company: SoloLearn

SoloLearn.com

Proximity is a key principle in web design that helps users quickly understand the relationship between page elements. For example on the SoloLearn website we can see how courses and tutorials are grouped by specific topics such as: website, mobile, data, etc. and this makes it easier to find the information on the page.

Repetition

Company: Duolingo

Duolingo.com

The principle of repetition in web design helps users recognize patterns and understand the structure of content by repeating elements such as buttons, navigation menus, titles and visual elements. Duolingo is a very good example of how repetition helps to make web design intuitive and easy to understand and use. The buttons and navigation menu are very simple and help to recognize the levels thanks to the colors and the repetitive format of the design.

Alignment

Company: Church of Jesus Christ of LDS

lcr.churchofjesuschrist.org

Alignment in web design is used to create a consistent and attractive appearance on your website. By aligning elements such as text, images, content blocks, forms and design elements. Each element is located within a specific and ordered space, with clean margins that help to better understand the website and gives it a cleaner appearance.

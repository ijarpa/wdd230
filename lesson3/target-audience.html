Target Audience

The target audience of the website of the Chamber of Southern Chile is companies interested in economic activities related to tourism and culture in the southern region of Chile, including local residents and potential investors seeking information about the resources, services and events that take place in the southern region of Chile.

Visitors to the site range in age from 25 to 65 years old, with different levels of education and income. Many of them will have experience in the business world, and may be looking for networking opportunities and resources to help them grow their businesses.

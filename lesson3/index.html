Site Purpose

The objective of the Chamber of Southern Chile's website is to serve as a hub for businesses and individuals in the Southern Chile region. The site aims to promote and provide information about local businesses, resources and networking opportunities. In addition, the site will showcase the region's unique attractions and culture, encouraging tourism and attracting new businesses to the area. The site seeks to facilitate economic growth and development in southern Chile, while serving as a valuable tool for its members and visitors.
